Payment Methods: Step-by-Step Guide

At Azurverde immigration Services, we provide two secure and reliable payment options to make your transactions as smooth as possible. Below are the detailed steps for each method:

1. Binance Payment Method

The Binance payment method is ideal for clients who prefer using cryptocurrency for their transactions. Here’s how you can make a payment through Binance:

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  • Download the Binance App: Visit the official website of Binance (binance.com) or your device’s app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store). Download and install the Binance app on your smartphone or tablet.
  • Register and Create an Account: Open the Binance app and tap on "Register." Follow the prompts to create an account by entering your email address or phone number and setting a strong password. Verify your account by completing the security verification process, which may include providing a government-issued ID and taking a selfie for identity verification.
  • Deposit Funds into Your Binance Account: Once your account is verified, log in to the app. Tap on the "Wallet" tab, select "Deposit," and choose your local currency. Follow the instructions to deposit funds from your bank account or other funding source into your Binance account.
  • Convert Your Local Currency to USD (or Desired Currency): Navigate to the "Trade" tab and select "Convert." Enter the amount of your local currency you wish to convert and select "USD" (or another preferred currency) as the target currency. Review the conversion details and confirm the transaction.
  • Transfer Funds to Azurverde immigration Services: After the conversion, go back to the "Wallet" tab, select "Withdraw," and choose "Send via Crypto Network." Enter the cryptocurrency wallet address provided by Azurverde Immigration Consulting and specify the amount to transfer. Double-check the details, including the wallet address and amount, to ensure they are correct. Confirm the transaction, and Binance will process the payment.
  • Confirmation and Support: After sending the payment, you will receive a confirmation from Binance. Please share this confirmation with us for verification. If you encounter any issues or need assistance during this process, our team is available to guide you step-by-step and ensure a smooth transaction.
2. Local Bank Account Payment Method

The local bank account payment method is a straightforward and secure option for clients who prefer traditional banking channels. Here’s how you can make a payment to our local bank account:

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  • Receive Bank Account Details: Contact Azurverde immigration Services to receive the local bank account details where the payment should be made. The bank account is owned by a trusted colleague who works in the financial department of the immigration commission in Canada. Although he resides in Canada, he maintains this local bank account specifically to facilitate payments.
  • Visit Your Bank or Use Online Banking: You can choose to visit your local bank branch or use your bank's online banking platform to make the payment. If you are using online banking, log in to your bank account through the official website or mobile app.
  • Initiate the Payment: Select the option to "Transfer Funds" or "Make a Payment." Enter the recipient's bank account details provided by us, including the bank name, account number, and any other required information.
  • Enter the Payment Amount: Specify the amount you wish to transfer in your local currency. Double-check all details to ensure the payment is directed to the correct account.
  • Complete the Transaction: Follow the instructions to complete the payment. Depending on your bank, you may need to provide a transaction PIN or use two-factor authentication to authorize the payment. Once the payment is processed, keep the transaction receipt or confirmation for your records.
  • Notify Azurverde immigration Services: After making the payment, notify us immediately by sending a copy of the payment receipt or confirmation. Our colleague will verify the receipt of funds in the local bank account and promptly transfer them to us in Canada.
  • Confirmation and Follow-Up: We will confirm receipt of the payment and keep you updated on the next steps of your process with us. Should you have any questions or need support during the payment process, we are here to assist you.
Security and Assurance

Both payment options are designed to be safe and secure:

  • Binance Payment Method: Offers a fast, secure, and modern way to transfer funds globally using cryptocurrency.
  • Local Bank Account Payment Method: Provides a traditional banking option with the safety of a bank-to-bank transfer, monitored by our trusted colleague in the financial department.

Choose the option that suits you best, and feel free to reach out to us for any help you may need during the payment process. Your satisfaction and security are our top priorities.
