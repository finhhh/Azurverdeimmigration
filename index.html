About Agency

Meet Christopher Bender, MBA, RCIC

Founder & CEO
Trust, respect, and a commitment to excellence

Having lived in and visited many regions of Canada during my lifetime, I realize every day how lucky I am to have grown up with all the advantages life in Canada has to offer. Growing up in Montreal, I was always surrounded by a wonderful diversity of languages , cultures and ideas, all with common goals like getting an education and a fulfilling career, getting married, having kids, buying a home and being part of a community. Today, I want to help others with their plans and dreams of coming to Canada
With over 20 years of experience in marketing and product development for big industrial companies, I know what it takes to create proposals and the importance of details and timelines. My clients know what they can expect from me: friendliness, honesty, empathy and good old fashioned hard work

I have been a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC R709171) since 2021 and I am recognized by the Québec Ministry of Immigration to submit immigration applications to Quebec & Other Provinces, in accordance with provincial law. I am uniquely qualified to assist with immigration processes across Canada. My recognition in Quebec allows me to provide specialized services for applications specific to this province, but I am fully authorized to assist individuals & families to submit applications to all provinces in Canada, adhering to provincial and federal laws.

Whether you're seeking permanent residency, temporary visas, work or study permits, or exploring immigration pathways to Quebec or other provinces, I am here to guide you every step of the way. I speak fluent English, and I am improving my French & Spanish every day. I look forward to working for you to get results, whatever your Canadian citizenship or immigration needs may be!
