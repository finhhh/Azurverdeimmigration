Spousal Sponsorship

Sponsor your spouse or common-law partner for permanent residency and start your life together in Canada.

Facts about Sponsor Eligibility

  • 18+ years old
  • Must not have been sponsored as a spouse within the last 5 years
  • Cannot be bankrupt, in prison, convicted of a serious offense, or under a removal order
  • No income requirement but must demonstrate an ability to financially care for your partner (and any dependents) for three years after they become a permanent resident.

Increase Chances of Approval

Couples must demonstrate to the immigration officer that you are a loving, committed couple and did not enter the relationship solely to obtain permanent resident status in Canada. Immigration officers will assess this component through reviewing your documents and through an interview process.

Officers look very carefully for signs of immigration fraud through phony relationships.

Here are some examples:

  • Communication between you and your partner
  • Personal statements and letters of support
  • Photos together and with family
  • Proof of visits, travel together
  • Joint documents (showing same address)
  • Financial Support like money transfer
